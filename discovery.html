Brothers: A Tale of Two Sonsmore_vert

Anthony's Recommendation

Brothers: A Tale of Two Sonsclose

Brothers has everything that I look for in a game: an interesting plot, a unique game mechanic, and a beautiful universe, among many other things. The first thing you notice about the game is the truly unique control system. The player controls two people simultaneously using the two analog sticks and just two buttons. This takes some getting used to but the game eases you into it before throwing tough puzzles that require some rather ambidextrous fingerwork at you, all the while providing you with a great soundtrack and one of the most beautiful and interesting surroundings. Overall, this game is one of the best I've ever played and would recommend wholeheartedly to anyone looking for an adventure of a lifetime.
